Why Choose UVMF?

We are a dedicated Microfinance Foundation focused on the economic growth of the underserved, driven by technology and transparency.

Our Vision

To be a catalyst in providing economic support to the underserved, realizing greater financial and community well-being through sustainable financial organizations.

To advance the monetary health of underserved Micro and Small entrepreneurs by supporting them in income-generation activities through vital financial intermediation.
