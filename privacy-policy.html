Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023 | Utthan Vision Microfinance Foundation

1. Introduction

Welcome to Utthan Vision Microfinance Foundation (UVMF). We are deeply committed to empowering rural India through accessible financial services, including small business loans and income-generating loans. As a trusted microfinance institution in India, we understand that protecting your privacy and financial data is paramount.

This Privacy Policy outlines how UVMF collects, uses, protects, and handles your personal and financial information when you use our website, mobile applications, or branch services. By engaging with our 100% cashless loan process, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with secure and efficient financial services with our rapid 3-day Turn Around Time (TAT), we collect specific types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, residential address, and contact details (phone number, email address).
  • KYC (Know Your Customer) Data: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, or other government-issued identification required by RBI guidelines.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, income proofs, existing loan statements, and transaction history required to assess your loan eligibility.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and website usage data collected when you browse our platform online.

3. How We Use Information

We strictly use your data to facilitate and improve our microfinance services. Your information is utilized to:

  • Process, underwrite, and disburse small loans online and offline securely.
  • Verify your identity and ensure compliance with regulatory KYC norms in India.
  • Manage your loan account, process digital EMI repayments, and maintain our 99.90% recovery track record.
  • Communicate with you regarding loan approvals, offers, policies, or grievance redressal.
  • Enhance our cloud-based AWS infrastructure for a smoother, faster customer experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Transparency is our core value. UVMF does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial data to any third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information under the following strict conditions:

  • Regulatory Bodies: With the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Credit Information Companies (like CIBIL, Equifax), or statutory authorities as mandated by Indian law.
  • Service Providers: With our trusted ITBS server administrators and AWS cloud providers who are contractually bound to keep your information confidential and secure.
  • Legal Compliance: If required to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of UVMF, our clients, or others.

5. Data Security Measures

We employ enterprise-grade security protocols to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our security framework includes:

  • Hosting all customer data on highly secure, encrypted Cloud-based AWS servers and specialized ITBS banking servers.
  • End-to-end encryption for all digital and 100% cashless transactions.
  • Strict role-based access controls ensuring only authorized UVMF personnel can access sensitive financial data.
  • Regular cybersecurity audits and compliance checks to maintain the highest standard of data protection in the microfinance sector.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website utilizes cookies—small text files stored on your device—to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand website traffic, remember your preferences, and optimize our platform for mobile and desktop users. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, doing so may affect the functionality of certain website features.

7. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal, KYC, and financial data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, and to comply with statutory retention periods mandated by Indian financial and tax regulations. Once the retention period expires, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

8. User Rights

As a valued UVMF customer, you possess specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the personal and loan data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You can request immediate corrections if you find any of your KYC or contact information to be inaccurate or outdated.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: If you have concerns about your data privacy, you can directly approach our dedicated grievance officers for swift resolution.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (such as government portals or payment gateways). Please note that UVMF does not control these external websites and is not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit.

10. Updates to Policy

UVMF reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our technology, services, or Indian regulatory requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the revised policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
