Utthan Vision Microfinance Foundation (UVMF)

About UVMF
Utthan Vision Microfinance Foundation (UVMF), true to the meaning of its name (‘easily accessible’), bridges the gap between people’s aspirations, wishes, 
and reality. UVMF is  registered under Pursuant to sub-section (2) of section 7 of the Companies Act, 2013 and rule 8 of the Companies (Incorporation) 
Rules, 2014 with rural and semi-rural financing at the center of its Micro  Loan , UVMF provides affordable financing with simple, no-hassle loan products. 
UVMF offer financial services and income generating Microloans.

By offering small ticket income generating loans to bottom of pyramid customers, it offers poor house-hold to increase their income and drastically enhance 
their livelihood.

We are serving in area and clients where it is not possible for the banks to provide financial services under the current market scenario. This distinctive 
characteristic of the last mile credit delivery system separates UVMF  from a Bank and/or other NBFCs.

Our Journey
Utthan Vision Microfinance Foundation (UVMF) was incorporated in 01/01/2016 . We started our operation from Varanasi  Head Quarter in 2025 with 
Micro Loan. Our Micro Loan vertical  became operational from October , 2025 through our first branch office in  Varanasi  (UP) . Within short span of 6 
months, we are now operational in Uttar Pradesh in 2 Districs ( Varanasi, Jaunpur) with 2 Branches.  

Achievements
The current total outstanding of the company stand today is – 1.25 CR  at present in Uttar Pradesh and around 500 + happy customers . Since beginning, 
UVMF is using technologies in very effective way and doing  cash and cashless collection/disbursement maintaining high level of security & data backups 
providing easy solution to its customer through dedicated online service. Since inception, UVMF remain profitable.
